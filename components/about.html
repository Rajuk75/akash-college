Our Story

Founded in 2010 by visionary educator Mr. Rakesh Verma, Akash Public School began with just 47 students and 5 teachers. Today, we stand as one of the most prestigious educational institutions in Lucknow, with over 1,200 students and 75 faculty members.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that equips students with intellectual growth, character development, and leadership skills necessary to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence that nurtures innovative thinkers, compassionate leaders, and responsible global citizens.
